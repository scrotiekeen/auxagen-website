# Auxano Agency — Department Structure

*Last updated: April 24, 2026*

---

## Public vs. Internal

This document captures the full department structure for Auxano Agency. Three departments are **public-facing** and appear on the website and in client-facing materials. Within Business Consulting, two **divisions — Sales and Marketing — are internal-only for now** and will be flipped to public once delivery process, pricing, and capacity are in place.

---

## Web & Software

We design, build, and maintain custom software and websites that give your business a working digital foundation. From front-end design to ongoing upkeep, we handle the build and keep it running so you don't have to.

**Services:**

- **Custom Software Development** — We build software tailored to your specific workflows and needs.
- **Website Design & Development** — Full custom sites built around your brand and business goals.
- **Front-End Design** — Clean, modern interfaces designed to convert and retain visitors.
- **Website Maintenance & Upkeep** — Ongoing updates, security patches, and performance monitoring.
- **SEO & GEO Optimization** — We optimize your site to rank in traditional search and generative engines.
- **Hosting & Infrastructure Management** — We handle the backend so your site stays fast and online.

## AI Strategy

We help you figure out where AI fits in your business, then we build and deploy it. This department covers both the strategic planning and the hands-on implementation of agents, bots, and automated workflows.

**Services:**

- **AI Readiness Audit** — We assess where your business stands and where AI can move the needle.
- **Opportunity Roadmap** — A prioritized plan of where to apply AI, in what order, and what it'll cost.
- **Custom Implementation Plan** — A step-by-step blueprint tailored to your tools, team, and timeline.
- **Tool & Vendor Selection** — We cut through the noise and match you to the right AI tools for your operation.
- **Workflow & Process Review** — We map your current workflows and identify what's ripe for automation or AI assist.
- **AI Agents & Bots** — Custom-built agents that handle tasks, answer questions, or run processes for you.
- **Phone Call Automation** — Voice bots that handle inbound or outbound calls at scale.
- **Cold Email Automation** — Automated outreach systems built to generate leads without manual effort.
- **Automated Workflows** — End-to-end automations that connect your tools and eliminate repetitive work.
- **Ongoing Advisory** — Retainer-based strategic guidance as your business and AI evolve.

## Business Consulting

Not every problem needs an AI solution, and we'll tell you when it doesn't. This department exists to meet you where you are — we'll help you find the right solution for your business, AI or otherwise, so the door stays open to work on whatever actually moves the needle.

**Services:**

- **Business Assessment** — End-to-end review of your operation to identify what's working, what's leaking, and where to focus.
- **Growth Strategy** — A prioritized plan for scaling revenue, based on your capacity and market.
- **Operational Review** — We map your processes and find the friction points.
- **Ongoing Advisory** — Retainer-based guidance as your business grows.

## Flip Signals — When to Make Sales & Marketing Public

Either condition triggers the move from internal to public:

1. **Delivery proof** — Service delivered informally for 2–3 clients, with documented process and pricing.
2. **Capacity** — Hours available (personal or contractor) to take on the work without cannibalizing the three live departments.

Until then, Business Consulting handles any sales or marketing needs that surface as custom engagements, and those engagements serve as unpaid market research for eventual service packaging.
